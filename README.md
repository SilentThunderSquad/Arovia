## ğŸ¯ About the Project

The **Doctor Recommendation System** is an intelligent healthcare platform designed to help patients find the most suitable doctors based on their medical needs, location, specialization, and availability. This system leverages modern technology to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers, ensuring quick and reliable access to medical consultation.

### Why This Project?

Finding the right doctor can be challenging, especially when dealing with specific medical conditions or seeking specialists. Our system aims to:
- Simplify the doctor search process
- Provide personalized recommendations based on patient needs
- Enable easy appointment scheduling
- Improve healthcare accessibility
- Reduce wait times for medical consultations
